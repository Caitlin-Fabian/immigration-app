You're Almost There!

You will now wait for a decision from USCIS based on your N-400 form. Here are your steps based on whether you are granted or denied.

Application Granted

Carry on to the next page!

Application Denied

This means the evidence on record determined you are not eligible for naturalization. This could be for two reasions:

Reason 1: You may need to provide additional documents or evidence through a Form N-14.

Reason 2: You failed the English or civic test, or both. USCIS will schedule for you to come back within 60-90 days of your first interview. You will only be tested on the part you failed. If you fail a second time, you're N-400 form will be denied.

You will receive a denial notice stating how to appeal the USCIS decision by filing Form N-366. This MUST be done within 30 days of the decision date, otherwise the denial decision is final.
